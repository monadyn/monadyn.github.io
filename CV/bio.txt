Huasong Shan received the PhD degree in computer engineering from Louisiana State University-Baton Rouge in 2017. He received his M.S. and B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology in China in 2003 and 2006, respectively. He has been working for JD.com American Technologies Corporation (Mountain View, CA) as Staff Scientist. His research interests are in the areas of distributed system,  application of AI on system and security, cloud computing, storage system, IoT etc.
